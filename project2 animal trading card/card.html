Lion

A lion’s heels don’t touch the ground when it walks and can run for short distances at 50 mph and leap as far as 36 feet.

  1. Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  2. Average Length: 2.7 meter
  3. Average Lifespan: 15 Years
  4. Habitat: Sahara in Africa

The lion (Panthera leo) is one of the big cats in the genus Panthera and a member of the family Felidae. The commonly used term African lion collectively denotes the several subspecies in Africa. With some males exceeding 250 kg (550 lb) in weight, it is the second-largest living cat after the tiger, barring hybrids like the liger.
